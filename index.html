About Me

Hi! I am Research Intern in the AVIZ team at INRIA, supervised by Petra Isenberg. My work focuses on Situated Visualization in Motion for Video games. I graduated from Politecnico di Milano in Computer Science and currently I am completing my double degree Master's program in Human-Computer Interaction & Design held by EIT Digital. I am passionate about video games and game design and I love working with Unity! I am also interested in web development, expecially front-end, data visualization, UX design and VR.
